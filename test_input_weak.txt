Standard Operating Procedure: Information Management

1. CONFIDENTIALITY AND ACCESS CONTROL

This hospital maintains confidentiality of all patient information and ensures privacy of records.
Authorized access is granted to designated staff. The hospital enforces role based access control.
Data security measures are in place. Password protection is enabled.

2. QUALITY MONITORING

The hospital identifies quality indicators. Monitoring is conducted through data collection.
Analysis of performance data is carried out periodically.
